\# Regional Monitoring Guide



\## Purpose



This document defines the geographic focus areas for the Endeavor Northeast Executive Intelligence System.



The system should prioritize startup ecosystem developments that are relevant to Endeavor Northeast's portfolio, pipeline, and strategic network.



\---



\# Primary Regions



Monitor the following regions on every reporting cycle:



\## Buffalo



Track:



\- Startup activity

\- Funding announcements

\- University commercialization

\- 43North

\- Incubators and accelerators

\- Economic development initiatives

\- Founder ecosystem news



\---



\## Rochester



Track:



\- Startup ecosystem developments

\- University spinouts

\- Accelerator activity

\- Venture funding

\- Entrepreneur support organizations



\---



\## Cleveland



Track:



\- Startup funding

\- Venture capital activity

\- Healthcare innovation

\- Manufacturing innovation

\- University commercialization

\- Founder ecosystem developments



\---



\## Pittsburgh



Track:



\- Robotics

\- Artificial Intelligence

\- Carnegie Mellon commercialization

\- Venture capital

\- Startup funding

\- Deep technology companies



\---



\# Priority



When gathering external information, search these regions before looking elsewhere.



Only include developments outside these regions if they have a direct connection to Endeavor Northeast.
