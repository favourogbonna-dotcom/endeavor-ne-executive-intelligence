\# Company Evaluation Framework



\## Purpose



This framework defines how Claude should evaluate whether a company update is important enough to include in an executive intelligence report.



Not every company update deserves executive attention.



Focus on significance, strategic relevance, and business impact.



\---



\# Evaluation Questions



For every company update, ask:



1\. Did something meaningful change?

2\. Does this affect the company's growth or trajectory?

3\. Does this matter to Endeavor Northeast?

4\. Would Cameron or Alan benefit from knowing this?



If the answer to most of these questions is "No," exclude the update.



\---



\# High-Value Company Updates



Always prioritize updates involving:



\- Funding announcements

\- New customers

\- Revenue milestones

\- Product launches

\- Strategic partnerships

\- Major hiring

\- Executive leadership changes

\- Market expansion

\- Accelerator acceptance

\- Awards and recognition

\- Acquisitions

\- Exit events



\---



\# Medium-Value Updates



Include only if they provide useful context:



\- Hiring growth

\- New office locations

\- Industry conference presentations

\- New advisory board members

\- Pilot programs

\- Research collaborations



\---



\# Low-Value Updates



Usually exclude:



\- Routine LinkedIn posts

\- Holiday greetings

\- Company anniversaries

\- General marketing posts

\- Team celebrations

\- Minor website changes

\- Routine event attendance



\---



\# Portfolio Companies



Portfolio companies receive the highest priority.



Report developments that demonstrate:



\- Growth

\- Traction

\- Risk

\- Investment activity

\- Strategic progress



\---



\# Pipeline Companies



Report updates that change the evaluation of the company, including:



\- Fundraising progress

\- Customer traction

\- Product maturity

\- Founder momentum

\- Market validation



\---



\# Capital Partners



Highlight developments involving:



\- New funds

\- Investment activity

\- Leadership changes

\- New investment themes

\- Partnerships with Endeavor



\---



\# Regional Ecosystem



Only include ecosystem updates that materially affect the startup community in:



\- Buffalo

\- Rochester

\- Cleveland

\- Pittsburgh



\---



\# Final Decision



Before including any company update, ask:



Does this help leadership make better decisions or stay informed about meaningful developments?



If not, leave it out.
