\# Daily Executive Slack Brief Routine



\## Schedule 



\- Monday–Friday

\- 8:00 AM Eastern



\---



\## Objective



Produce a concise morning briefing for Endeavor Northeast leadership.



\---



\## Step 1 — Collect Information



Gather data in the following priority order:



1\. Monday.com

2\. Slack

3\. Gmail

4\. Google Calendar

5\. Notion

6\. Public Web Sources



\---



\## Step 2 — Analyze



Evaluate findings using:



\- company-evaluation.md

\- news-evaluation-framework.md

\- research-standards.md



Discard low-value information.



\---



\## Step 3 — Prioritize



Order information as:



1\. Portfolio Companies

2\. Pipeline Companies

3\. Capital Partners

4\. Regional Ecosystem

5\. General Startup News



\---



\## Step 4 — Draft



Follow:



\- prompts/daily-slack-brief.md



Use:



\- templates/daily-slack-update.md



\---



\## Step 5 — Review



Verify:



\- Writing Style

\- Accuracy

\- Dates

\- Sources



Remove duplicate information.



\---



\## Step 6 — Deliver



Send as a Slack message.



If no updates exist for a section, omit that section.



Never fabricate information.
