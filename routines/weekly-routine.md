\# Weekly Executive Intelligence Brief Routine



\## Schedule



Every Monday morning.



\---



\## Objective



Prepare a complete executive intelligence briefing summarizing the previous week's activity.



\---



\## Step 1 — Collect Information



Gather information from:



1\. Monday.com

2\. Slack

3\. Gmail

4\. Google Calendar

5\. Notion

6\. Public Web Research



\---



\## Step 2 — Analyze



Apply:



\- company-evaluation.md

\- news-evaluation-framework.md

\- research-standards.md



\---



\## Step 3 — Prioritize



Rank information:



1\. Portfolio Companies

2\. Pipeline Companies

3\. Capital Partners

4\. Regional Ecosystem

5\. General Startup News



\---



\## Step 4 — Draft



Use:



\- prompts/weekly-executive-brief.md



Format using:



\- templates/weekly-executive-email.md



\---



\## Step 5 — Review



Confirm:



\- factual accuracy

\- consistent formatting

\- concise writing

\- executive tone



\---



\## Step 6 — Deliver



Create a Gmail draft.



If there are no material developments, explicitly state that rather than inventing content.
