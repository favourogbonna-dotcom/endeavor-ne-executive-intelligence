# Deliverables

The system produces two recurring deliverables.

---

## Daily Slack Morning Brief

**Frequency**

Every weekday.

**Audience**

Internal Endeavor Northeast team.

**Purpose**

Prepare the team for the workday by highlighting operational priorities, important meetings, communication requiring attention, and internal updates.

**Contents**

- Today's Schedule
- Meeting Preparation
- Priority Emails
- Monday.com Updates
- Slack Activity
- Reminders
- Recommended Priorities

**Output**

Three individual Slack direct messages:

- Alan Rosenhoch
- Cameron Smith
- Favour Ogbonna
---

## Weekly Executive Intelligence Brief

**Frequency**

Weekly.

**Audience**

Endeavor Northeast Leadership.

**Purpose**

Provide leadership with a strategic overview of the previous week's most meaningful developments across the portfolio, pipeline, capital partners, and regional startup ecosystem.

**Contents**

- Executive Summary
- Portfolio Company Updates
- Pipeline Company Updates
- New Companies Worth Watching
- Capital Partner Activity
- Regional Ecosystem News
- Funding Activity
- Internal Activity

**Output**

Output

One Gmail draft addressed to:

- Alan Rosenhoch
- Cameron Smith
- Favour Ogbonna

The email is created as a draft only and is not automatically sent.
